HTD(How To Do)

HTD site is where I plan and clean up 'How I will achieve my goals in the future?'.
It's based on the methods of the book, "The Power of Very Small Repetition."
Do you always set goals and give up halfway?
This site will help you make more successful plans.

Advantages of this Site
1. Improve achievement rate while planning goals in more detail.
2. Users can easily view detailed goals based on criteria such as difficulty level and importance.
3. To motivate users, there is an empty space where they write down the rewards to themselves they achieve certain goals.
4. You can select and use various lists depending on the period.
5. The chart shows the percentage of achievement achieved by various objectives.
